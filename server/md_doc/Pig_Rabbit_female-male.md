### Potential Challenges & Solutions (Premium Content)

#### Conflict 1: Communication Differences

##### Severity
17

##### Description

#### Challenge 4: Decision-Making Speed

##### Severity
50

##### Description
The Snake's preference for thorough analysis and the Rabbit's desire for quicker, intuition-based decisions can create friction in time-sensitive business situations. The Rabbit may feel the Snake's deliberation causes missed opportunities, while the Snake may feel rushed into decisions without adequate information. This can be particularly problematic in fast-moving markets or when responding to competitive threats.

##### Resolution
Create a tiered decision-making framework based on decision importance and time sensitivity. For routine operational decisions, empower the partner with relevant expertise to decide quickly. For strategic decisions, set reasonable time limits that allow for analysis without paralysis. Use decision-making tools like SWOT analysis or decision matrices to structure discussions and move toward conclusions more efficiently. Agree on specific criteria that trigger fast-track decision processes.

#### Challenge 5: Conflict Avoidance

##### Severity
35

##### Description

Issues may simmer beneath the surface, leading to resentment or passive-aggressive behavior. Important strategic disagreements may go unresolved, creating ambiguity in business direction.
