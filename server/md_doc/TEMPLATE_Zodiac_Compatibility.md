### Gender Combinations
- **male-male**: Two males
- **female-female**: Two females
- **male-female**: One male and one female (covers both male-female and female-male)

### Score Guidelines
- **90-100**: Perfect/Exceptional compatibility
- **75-89**: Excellent/Very strong match
- **60-74**: Good/Solid compatibility
- **40-59**: Fair/Moderate compatibility
- **0-39**: Poor/Challenging match

### Severity Guidelines (for Conflicts)
- **0-25**: Minor - Easy to manage
- **26-50**: Moderate - Requires attention
- **51-75**: Significant - Needs active management
- **76-100**: Major - Requires professional guidance

### Content Length Guidelines
- **Overall Assessment**: 200-300 words
- **Premium Section Content**: 150-250 words each
- **Conflict Description**: 50-100 words
- **Resolution Advice**: 50-100 words
- **Highlights**: 3 concise bullet points per section
