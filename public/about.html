About Us

Welcome to LaraK's Oracle — the cosmic crossroads where ancient wisdom meets modern spirit.

Who We Are

LaraK's Limited Company is officially registered in Auckland, New Zealand, with our creative & operations hub in Los Angeles, USA. We established LaraK's Oracle to share two powerful traditions: the mystical art of Tarot, and the ancient Chinese Zodiac wisdom — guiding seekers through life's questions, challenges, and turning points.

What We Do

Under LaraK's Oracle, we run two main wings:

LaraK's Oracle Tarot

Dedicated to Tarot card reading, education, and guidance. Through personalized readings, insightful interpretations, and thoughtful content, we help you explore your inner self, navigate relationships, career, emotions and spiritual growth.

LaraK's Oracle Zodiac

Where Chinese Zodiac astrology and divination meet modern life. We offer consultations and insights rooted in traditional Chinese zodiac wisdom, helping you understand the energy patterns, personality traits, and life cycles connected to your zodiac sign.

In addition to our reading and consulting services, we also offer a curated selection of Tarot- and Zodiac-inspired gifts and merchandise — from stylish decks to zodiac-themed accessories — for those who want to carry the magic into everyday life.

Why We Exist / Our Mission

In a world buzzing with noise and unpredictability, many of us long for clarity, meaning and connection — to ourselves, to the universe, and to something beyond the mundane. Our mission is simple:

  • To empower individuals to access ancient metaphysical wisdom in a respectful, modern way;
  • To offer compassionate, honest, and insightful guidance without judgment;
  • To build a community where spiritual curiosity is welcomed, shared, and celebrated.

We believe Tarot and Zodiac aren't just tools for fortune-telling — they're mirrors to the inner self, bridges between cultures, and sources of personal insight.

Our Story

LaraK's Oracle grew from a passion for ancient wisdom and a desire to help people navigate uncertain times. From a humble beginning in New Zealand, we expanded our reach to Los Angeles to bring a truly international perspective. Over time, as more people from different backgrounds connected with our vision, we realized this wasn't just a hobby — it was a calling. Thus LaraK's Limited Company was born, combining Eastern and Western mystical traditions into a single brand dedicated to spiritual growth and self-discovery.
